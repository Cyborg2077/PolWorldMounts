Fenglope and Rushoar Mounts Mod - Version Updates

Note: If you equip Water Walking Boots, the mounts can also walk on water!

v0.1 Update:
1. Added Fenglope mount! Crafting recipe: 10 Wood + 10 Stone + 5 Sapphire
2. When equipped with a Cloud in a Bottle, the Fenglope can perform a double jump.

v0.2 Update:
1. Right-click to dash with a 10-second cooldown.
2. During dashing, the Fenglope deals 50 collision damage and knockback.

v0.3 Update:
1. No need for Cloud in a Bottle to perform a double jump.
2. Normal speed increased to 77; dash speed remains the same.

v0.5 Update:
1. Added Rushoar mount! Crafting recipe: 10 Wood + 10 Stone + 5 Sapphire
2. The Rushoar deals 50 collision damage and knockback while dashing and destroys ordinary trees in its path (excluding special trees like VanityTreeSakura).

幻兽帕鲁坐骑Mod，现已添加了云海鹿、草莽猪坐骑
如果你装备水上漂靴，坐骑同样可以水上漂

v0.1更新
1. 添加了云海鹿走起，合成配方：10个木材 + 10个石头 + 5个蓝玉
2. 当你装备了云瓶，那么云海鹿可以进行二段跳

v0.2更新
1. 单击鼠标右键进行冲刺，冲刺拥有10秒CD
1. 新增冲刺状态下50点碰撞伤害和击退

v0.3更新
1. 不再需要云朵瓶来进行二段跳
2. 常态速度提高为77，冲刺速度不变

v0.5更新
1. 添加了草莽猪坐骑，合成配方：10个木材 + 10个石头 + 5个蓝玉
2. 草莽猪冲刺状态下造成50点碰撞伤害以及击退，同时会摧毁沿途路上的普通树木（一些特殊的树木除外，例如樱花树、宝石树等）
