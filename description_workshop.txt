Polworld Mounts Mod - Version Updates

Current mounts added: Fenglope, Rushoar, Jetragon

Version 0.7 Update
1. Jetragon now has three new abilities: Flare Storm (Z key), Beam Comet (X key), and Dragon Meteor (C key). Flare Storm will attract nearby hostile NPCs, Dragon Meteor will fire in the direction of the mouse, and Beam Comet will also fire in the direction of the mouse and then automatically track nearby enemies.
2. Fenglope and Rashoar's dashes are now bound to the Z key.
3. More mounts and skills will be added in future updates.

v0.6 Update:
1. Added Jetragon mount. Crafting recipe: 10 Lunar Bars + 20 Silk + 5 Sapphires. Requires Polworld Device Workbench.
2. Added Polworld Device Workbench. Crafting materials: 30 Wood + 10 Silk + 10 Sapphires.
3. Modified the crafting recipes for Rushoar and Fenglope:
    - Rushoar: 10 Wood + 10 Stone + 5 Sapphires + 10 Leather + 5 Vertebrae. Right-click to charge, destroying normal trees along the way (excluding some special trees like sakura trees).
    - Fenglope: 20 Hallowed Bars + 5 Souls of Flight + 5 Souls of Light + 5 Sapphires. Can perform a double jump, and right-click to dash.

幻兽帕鲁坐骑Mod，现已添加了云海鹿、草莽猪、空涡龙

v0.7更新
1. 为空涡龙增加了三个技能：烈焰风暴（Z键）、光束彗星（X键）、龙彗星（C键）。烈焰风暴会吸附周围的敌对NPC、龙彗星会沿鼠标方向发射、光束彗星会沿鼠标方向发射，随后自动追踪周围敌怪。
2. 云海鹿和草莽猪的冲刺改为Z键。
3. 后续会添加更多坐骑，以及更多的技能。


v0.6更新
1. 增加了空涡龙坐骑，合成配方：10个夜明锭 + 20个丝绸 + 5个蓝玉，需要帕鲁装置制作台
2. 增加了帕鲁装置制作台，合成材料：30个木头 + 10个丝绸 + 10个蓝玉
3. 修改了草莽猪和云海鹿的合成配方
    - 草莽猪：10个木头 + 10个石头 + 5个蓝玉 + 10个皮革 + 5个椎骨，单击右键进行冲刺，冲刺会摧毁沿途路上的普通树木（一些特殊树木如樱花树等除外）
    - 云海鹿：20个神圣锭 + 5个飞翔之魂 + 5个光明之魂 + 5个蓝玉，可以进行二段跳，单击右键进行冲刺
