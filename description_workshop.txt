Polworld Fenglope Mod

Crafting Materials: 10 Wood + 10 Stone + 5 Sapphires
Features:

Double Jump Compatibility: If you equip items like the Cloud in a Bottle or any other gear that grants extra jumps, the Cloud Deer will be able to utilize these additional jumps as well.
Water Walking Ability: When wearing Water Walking Boots, the Cloud Deer gains the ability to traverse across water surfaces without sinking.
